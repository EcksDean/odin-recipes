Strawberry Milk Tea With Boba Pearls

Original recipe by Therese Chua from Honest Food Talks

Description

Our strawberry milk tea recipe is a healthier and cheaper alternative to the boba shop! Using fresh strawberries, milk and tea, make our strawberry bubble tea recipe!

Ingredients

  • Strawberry
  • Tea bags
  • Milk
  • Sugar or honey

Directions

  1. Steep the tea bags according to the type of leaves and strength desired. Generally, if you steep them longer, you will have a stronger flavour.
  2. Combine the strawberries with milk or yoghurt in a blender and blend well.
  3. Add your sweetener of choice and adjust the taste based on your preference.
  4. Serve the strawberry milk tea in a glass and enjoy!
