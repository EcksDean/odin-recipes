Jasmine Boba Tea

Original recipe by Oh, How Civilized

Author: Jee Choe

Ingredients

Jasmine Green Milk Tea

  • 3 jasmine green tea sachets
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup ice
  • ⅓ cup whole milk

Tapioca Pearls

  • 2 cups water
  • ¼ cup tapioca balls

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 cup of water. If using an electric kettle with a temperature setting, set the water to 175°F. Use filtered water if possible.
  2. Steep tea sachets in hot water for 4 minutes.
  3. Discard tea sachets and stir in sugar. Let tea cool.
  4. Boil water and add tapioca balls. Boil water in a saucepan on the stovetop then add tapioca balls. Cook on medium-high heat for 8-10 minutes. Stir occasionally. Tapioca balls should be soft all the way through.
  5. Strain tapioca balls.
  6. Put tapioca balls into a cup. Add ice, tea, and milk. Use an extra wide straw to drink.
